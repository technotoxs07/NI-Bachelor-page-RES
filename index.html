About

National Infotech is a college that offers programs in the field of computer science and information technology. These programs are designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary for a career in the IT sector.

Our College


National Infotech is a renowned educational institution dedicated to providing high-quality education in the field of computer science and information technology. Established with the vision of nurturing IT professionals, the college has a strong commitment to excellence and innovation in education..

National Infotech's vision is to produce highly skilled IT professionals who can contribute to the growth and development of the IT industry. Its mission is to provide a dynamic and conducive learning environment that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving..

Courses

Specifically, it provides education for B.Sc. CSIT (Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology) and BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) students affiliated to TRIBHUVAN UNIVERSITY.

+2 science

National Infotech College offers a comprehensive +2 Science program tailored to prepare students for further studies in science, engineering, medicine, and other related fields. This program is designed to foster critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a deep understanding of scientific principles.

Bsc.csit

This program is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of computer science and information technology. It covers a wide range of subjects, including programming, data structures, database management, web development, and networking.

BCA

The BCA program at National Infotech is tailored to prepare students for a career in software development, application design, and IT management. It emphasizes practical skills and hands-on experience, ensuring that graduates are well-prepared for the IT sector.
